Our Mission

Our mission is to provide the best veterinary care we can for our clients' pets through holistic methods, utilizing raw food diet, supported by natural medicines and treatments like herbals, homeopathics, accupunture, and neutraceauticals when needed, and, if needed, pharmaceuticals, to support your pet's health and healing.

Our Vision

Our vision is a place where no animal is neglected, or abused. We offer our clients the highest quality care at the lowest possible price we can afford to give them. We offer low cost spay and neuter services for strays to help keep the population down, we work with the community to enforce a trap neuter/spay release program, and we offer food and shelter services for strays through the community.

Our Values

Through our high quality, low cost approach to vet care, we aim to foster loyalty to the community. We believe pets are an important part of the community and family, and as such deserve respect and care that all beings should deserve.
